About Me

Hello world! I'm Jacopo Bonato an experimental physicist (Master's degree from the University of Trento) and an Artificial Intelligence researcher (Ph.D. in Data Science and Computation at the University of Bologna and IIT). My research and work experiences were focused on computational neuroscience, information theory machine learning, and deep learning. Besides my academic and scientific interests, I love music. I had my academy diploma in classical guitar at the Conservatory of Music of Trento. Finally, I love sports and I practice a lot of activities: running, swimming, tennis and kitesurfing. Feel free to reach out if you have any questions.
